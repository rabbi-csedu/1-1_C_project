GAMEPLAY:
	1. Press the SPACE button to Shoot Arrow.
	2. From MEDIUM level the target board will start moving.
	3. In the HARD level arrow velocity will be changing!!! So, be carefull.

SCORING:
	1. If you hit the YELLOW portion, you will get 10 points and a BONUS arrow!!!
	2. If you hit the RED portion, you will get 8 points.
	3. If you hit the BLUE, BLACK or WHITE portion, you will get 6, 4 or 2 respectively.
	4. If you can not hit any portion on the target board, you will get a penalty and lose an arrow!!!

SHOP:
	You can also select character from the SHOP !!! So, before start playing the game, go to SHOP 
	and choose your character.
